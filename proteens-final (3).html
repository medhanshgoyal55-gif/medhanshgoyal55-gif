System Flow

How It Works

Fully real-time. No refresh needed. Every action flows automatically across the entire platform.

01

Academy Registers & Coaches Join

Academies sign up with verified information and onboard coaches. Players browse and register for trials based on location and sport.

02

Coach Enters Match Data

During or after a match, the coach inputs live events into ProScore — runs, goals, points, fouls, everything tracked in real time.

03

ProScore Processes Everything

The ProScore engine validates, structures, and stores every event against sport-specific rules — accurate, instant, tamper-proof.

04

Dashboards Update Instantly

The Academy dashboard, Player profile, and Coach analytics all update simultaneously. Every stat visible the moment it happens.

05

Players Own Their Performance

Players access their full match history, progress graphs, and stat breakdowns. Data-driven growth, owned by the athlete.

Questions

FAQ

What is ProScore and how does it work?
+
ProScore is the real-time scoring and statistics engine that powers ProTeens. Coaches enter match events and ProScore processes them instantly — updating player stats, academy dashboards, and match history simultaneously, with no refresh needed.
Which sports have full scoring support?
+
Cricket, Football, Basketball, Badminton, and Tennis have complete match engines with live scoring, match rules, timers, player statistics, and match history. The remaining 10 activities support registration, trials, and academy presence only.
How does ProTeens eliminate biased ratings?
+
ProTeens' philosophy is simple: no remarks, no opinions, no biased ratings. Every metric in the system comes directly from real match events entered by coaches through ProScore. Your stats speak for themselves.
How are academies verified?
+
All academies must submit authentic, official details during registration. Any fake names, duplicate identities, or misleading information results in immediate suspension or permanent removal from the platform.
Can players track their own progress?
+
Yes. Players have their own profile with full match history, stat breakdowns, and progress analytics. Data is owned by the player and reflects real match performance tracked by ProScore.
How does ProTeens help students discover academies?
+
ProTeens brings academies, coaching institutes, and skill centers into one organized space, making it easy for students to explore opportunities based on their interests, goals, and location — eliminating the confusion of scattered information.
